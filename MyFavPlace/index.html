La Jolla, California

La Jolla is a magical place I would live every summer because I'm a teacher and I have off every summer. Unfortunately, that stopped when my fiance got a full-time job. She ruined my magical summers of surfing and burritos.... La Jolla is a hilly, seaside community that has awesome restaurants and the University of San Diego. Almost everyone in La Jolla speaks Spanish and English since it's very close to Mexico.
